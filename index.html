About

I am Romanian, based in Amsterdam. I studied law at Northeastern University London and I am now completing an LLM in International Trade and Investment Law at the University of Amsterdam, with a thesis on how EU AI Act regulation interacts with investment protection standards for investors in AI companies.

Alongside that I have been doing editorial research at TenNine VC, writing on deep tech ecosystems and the investment landscape. Before Amsterdam I ran internal relations for the League of Romanian Students in the UK, organized an annual conference at the Romanian Embassy in London, and managed immigration compliance for foreign nationals in Bucharest. I also organized FoundHers, a women in leadership panel at UvA, and published a case note in Concurrences Bulletin on a Romanian competition law decision.

The thread across all of it is the same: I take situations that are complicated or unclear and work out what is actually going on and what to do about it. That is what I am building a career around.
